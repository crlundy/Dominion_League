A free tournament for competitive Dominion, open to players of all levels.
Sign up

Each season of the Dominion League is a round robin tournament that lasts 6 weeks. Competitors are placed into tiers based on their level and compete within the tiers to promote to a higher tier, or demote to a lower tier. The tournament is hosted at the Dominion Discord server. It is a fun and friendly competition where you can learn more about the game, play competitive Dominion and make friends.
